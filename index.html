Thunderbird Setup for demo@chhunmeyling.com

Step 1: Download Thunderbird

Get Thunderbird from the official website below.

Download Thunderbird

Step 2: Email Setup

Run the installer and follow the prompts to install.

Open Thunderbird and select "Set up an existing email account".

Your Name: Demo User
Email: demo@chhunmeyling.com
Password: [Your Password]

  • Incoming: IMAP, mail.chhunmeyling.com, Port: 993, SSL/TLS
  • Outgoing: SMTP, mail.chhunmeyling.com, Port: 465, SSL/TLS
  • Username: demo@chhunmeyling.com

Click "Done" and Thunderbird will complete the setup.

Step 3: Test Email

Click "Write", send an email to yourself, and verify receipt.

If the email appears in your Inbox, everything is working.

Step 4: Explore Features

Go to "Account Settings" > "Signature" to set your signature.

Right-click your account > New Folder to organize your inbox.

Use "Message Filters" to auto-organize emails by subject, sender, etc.
